> The platform engineering division of [BrazilCo](https://github.com/BrazilCo) — building scalable, secure, and production-grade infrastructure for Brazilian and global enterprises.

---

## 🏢 Who We Are

**BrazilCo** is a Brazilian software engineering and technology company building end-to-end digital solutions — from enterprise systems and cloud-native platforms to cutting-edge AI products.

**BrazilCo Platforms** is the dedicated platform engineering division within BrazilCo. We design, build, and operate the foundational infrastructure that supports all engineering teams and products across the company and its clients.

We bring together **DevOps excellence** and **cloud-native engineering** to deliver platforms that are reliable, secure, and ready for scale from day one.

---

## 🧱 Our Engineering Domains

### ☁️ Cloud & Infrastructure

Building cloud-native foundations that scale with the business.

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Infrastructure as Code** | Terraform and CloudFormation modules for reproducible, auditable cloud environments |
| **Kubernetes & Containers** | EKS and GKE cluster management, Helm charts, and workload orchestration |
| **Networking & Security** | VPCs, IAM, service mesh, and zero-trust network policies |
| **Cost Management (FinOps)** | Resource optimization, tagging strategies, and cloud cost governance |

### 🚀 CI/CD & Developer Experience

Enabling fast, safe, and consistent software delivery across all teams.

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Pipeline Templates** | Reusable GitHub Actions workflows for build, test, and deploy |
| **GitOps** | Argo CD-based delivery with automated sync and rollback |
| **Progressive Delivery** | Canary releases, feature flags, and blue/green deployments |
| **Developer Portals** | Internal tooling and self-service platforms for engineering teams |

### 🔒 Security & Governance

Embedding security and compliance at every layer of the platform.

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Secure Code Orchestration** | Automated scanning with Trivy, Semgrep, and OWASP Dependency-Check |
| **Policy as Code** | OPA/Gatekeeper policies and ADRs governing platform standards |
| **Audit & Compliance** | Centralized logging, audit trails, and regulatory controls |
| **Secrets Management** | Vault integration and secrets rotation across environments |

### 📊 Observability & Reliability

Making systems understandable and resilient in production.

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Monitoring** | Prometheus, Grafana dashboards, and alerting for all platform services |
| **Distributed Tracing** | OpenTelemetry instrumentation across microservices |
| **Incident Response** | Runbooks, on-call practices, and post-mortem culture |
| **SLOs & SLAs** | Service level objectives and error budget tracking |

## 🎯 Our Engineering Principles

- **Platform-first** — every decision is made with reliability and developer experience at its core
- **Engineering excellence** — clean infrastructure, automated testing, and continuous delivery
- **Production-grade** — reliability, security, and scalability are non-negotiable
- **GitOps by default** — all changes are versioned, reviewed, and auditable
- **Shift-left security** — security and compliance are integrated from design to deployment
